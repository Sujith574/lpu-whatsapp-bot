CGPA & GRADING SYSTEM (2025–26)
========================================

FORMULA:
CGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) / Σ(Credits)

GRADE POINTS:
O = 10  
A+ = 9  
A = 8  
B+ = 7  
B = 6  
C = 5  
D = 4  
E/F = Fail (0)

IMPROVEMENT:
• Reappear improves only end-term marks.
• CGPA improves only if course grade improves.

NORMALIZATION:
• Marks may be normalized section-wise.

TRANSCRIPTS:
• Download from UMS.
• Official transcript available from Registrar office.
