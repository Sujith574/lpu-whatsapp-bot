LPU HOSTEL ATTENDANCE & TIMINGS  
(Official Rules – 2025–26)

-------------------------------------------------------
1. HOSTEL ATTENDANCE TIMINGS
-------------------------------------------------------
All hostel students MUST be inside their allocated rooms during evening attendance.

ATTENDANCE TIME BY MONTH:
• 01 May – 31 Oct → 08:00 PM
• 01 Nov – 28 Feb → 07:00 PM
• 01 Mar – 30 Apr → 07:30 PM

Attendance is taken by **physical verification** by hostel wardens.

-------------------------------------------------------
2. ABSENT RULE
-------------------------------------------------------
A student is marked **ABSENT** if:
• They are NOT on approved hostel leave, AND  
• They are NOT present in the hostel during attendance time.

If marked ABSENT → strict disciplinary action is taken.

-------------------------------------------------------
3. LATE NIGHT ENTRY RULES
-------------------------------------------------------
• If a student returns to campus **after attendance time**, it is marked as a **Late-Night Entry**.
• Late-night entry is recorded by security staff at the main gate.
• Disciplinary action is taken depending on frequency and reason.

Note:
Having approved leave does NOT allow late entry after attendance time.

-------------------------------------------------------
4. OVERSTAY RULE
-------------------------------------------------------
A student is considered **OVERSTAY** if:
• They do not return before the end time of their approved leave.

Consequences:
• Warning or disciplinary action  
• Parents informed  
• Warden may restrict future leaves  

Students must extend or modify leave immediately if they cannot return on time.

-------------------------------------------------------
5. SILENT / STUDY HOURS
-------------------------------------------------------
Silent Hours: **10:00 PM – 06:00 AM**

Rules:
• No student is allowed to roam in corridors after 10 PM.
• Maintain complete silence in rooms and corridors.
• Noise, loud talking, calls, meetings, and music are not allowed.

Purpose:
To ensure a proper study and sleep environment for all hostel residents.

-------------------------------------------------------
6. ENTRY/EXIT RULES
-------------------------------------------------------
• Students must follow all security instructions.
• Movement is recorded via turnstile + biometric card.
• Using another student’s ID card is strictly prohibited.

-------------------------------------------------------
7. GENERAL DISCIPLINE FOR ATTENDANCE
-------------------------------------------------------
• Students must return to hostel before attendance.
• No one is allowed to stay back in hostel during class hours unless:
  – Medical condition  
  – Warden-approved permission  
• Parents are informed regularly about attendance-related issues.
