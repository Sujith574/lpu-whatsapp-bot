LPU TRANSPORT RULES (2025–26)
====================================

BUS PASS:
• Mandatory for using university buses.
• Available via UMS → Transport.

TIMINGS:
• Fixed schedule per route.
• Students must reach stop 10 minutes early.

MISCONDUCT:
• Fighting, damaging seats, or misbehavior → disciplinary action.

RULES:
• Valid ID card required.
• No standing in running bus.
• No eating or loud music inside bus.
