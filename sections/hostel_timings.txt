HOSTEL TIMINGS – LOVELY PROFESSIONAL UNIVERSITY (Boys & Girls)

-------------------------------------
1. HOSTEL IN-TIME (EVENING ENTRY)
-------------------------------------
Applicable for both Boys & Girls hostels:

• 01 May – 31 Oct → 08:00 PM
• 01 Nov – 28 Feb → 07:00 PM
• 01 Mar – 30 Apr → 07:30 PM

Students must be inside their allotted hostel rooms by this time.
Wardens will take physical attendance every day.

-------------------------------------
2. HOSTEL ATTENDANCE RULES
-------------------------------------
• Attendance is taken through physical verification by warden.
• Student must be inside their own hostel room during attendance.
• If absent without approved leave → Marked “ABSENT”
  → Strict disciplinary action will apply.

-------------------------------------
3. LATE-NIGHT ENTRY
-------------------------------------
If a student returns to campus AFTER the hostel attendance time:

• Entry is allowed only through Main Gate.
• Security marks it as “Late Entry”.
• Disciplinary action will be taken.
• Repeated late entries lead to strict actions by wardens.

-------------------------------------
4. LEAVE START TIMING
-------------------------------------
• Students can start their DAY/NIGHT leave after 05:00 AM.

-------------------------------------
5. RETURN TIMING FOR NIGHT LEAVE
-------------------------------------
For a night leave (1 day):

• Student MUST return AFTER 05:00 AM on the next day.
• They must report to hostel BEFORE the evening attendance time.

-------------------------------------
6. OVERSTAY (STAYING BEYOND LEAVE TIME)
-------------------------------------
• Staying outside after the approved leave end-time = “Overstay”
• Student must extend their leave in UMS.
• Disciplinary action for unapproved overstays.

-------------------------------------
7. SILENCE / STUDY HOURS
-------------------------------------
• Silence Hours: 10:00 PM – 06:00 AM
Rules:
• No roaming in corridors 
• No loud music or noise
• Students should stay inside rooms

-------------------------------------
8. MOVEMENT RESTRICTIONS
-------------------------------------
• Students cannot enter hostels other than their own.
• Movement outside hostel is restricted after attendance.
• Girls Hostel: Movement STRICTLY restricted after attendance except:
  - Extended Leave (within campus areas) – Max 3 hours
  - Only allowed in designated zones

-------------------------------------
9. VISITORS
-------------------------------------
• Visitors allowed only with a pre-approved Online Parent Pass.
• Timings:
  – Working Days: 04:00 PM – 07:00 PM
  – Sundays/Holidays: 09:00 AM – 07:00 PM
• Meetings only in visitor rooms, not inside hostel rooms.

-------------------------------------
10. MESS TIMINGS
-------------------------------------
Working Days:
• Breakfast: 07:15 AM – 09:30 AM
• Lunch:     11:30 AM – 03:00 PM
• Snacks:    04:45 PM – 06:00 PM
• Dinner:    07:30 PM – 09:30 PM

Sundays/Holidays:
• Breakfast: 08:00 AM – 10:00 AM
• Lunch:     12:30 PM – 03:00 PM
• Snacks:    04:45 PM – 06:00 PM
• Dinner:    07:30 PM – 09:30 PM

-------------------------------------
11. IMPORTANT NOTES
-------------------------------------
• ID card mandatory for mess entry.
• Food is NOT served in rooms (except for sick students with permission).
• Students should never argue with mess staff—contact warden instead.
