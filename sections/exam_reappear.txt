EXAM & REAPPEAR RULES (2025–26)
=====================================

REAPPEAR EXAM:
• Fee: ₹500 per course.
• Only end-term marks change.
• Internal marks remain same.
• No second chance if a student misses reappear exam.

ELIGIBILITY:
• Must have cleared prerequisites.
• Must not be under debarment/UMC punishment.

ADMIT CARD:
• Visible only if:
  – Fee fully paid
  – No disciplinary block
  – Attendance criteria met

EXAM RULES:
• ID card + Admit card mandatory.
• Phones, smartwatches, electronic gadgets prohibited.
• Late entry beyond 15 mins strictly not allowed.
• UMC leads to cancellation + disciplinary action.

SPECIAL REAPPEAR:
• Allowed only in medical emergencies or approved reasons.
• Requires official documents + approval.
