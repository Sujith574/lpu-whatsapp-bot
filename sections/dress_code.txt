LPU DRESS CODE RULES (2025–26)
======================================

FORMAL UNIFORM:
• Mandatory Monday to Friday.
• Includes:
  – Formal shirt + trousers
  – Tie (where applicable)
  – Blazer (winter)
  – Formal shoes

CASUAL WEAR:
• Allowed only on Saturday & Sunday.

RESTRICTIONS:
• Shorts, sleeveless tops, ripped jeans not allowed in academic areas.
• No slippers/sandals inside academic blocks.
• No inappropriate prints or text on clothing.

LABS/SPECIAL AREAS:
• Proper lab coats must be worn in:
  – Science labs
  – Engineering workshops
  – Pharmacy labs

VIOLATION:
• Student asked to leave class.
• Frequent violations may lead to disciplinary action.
