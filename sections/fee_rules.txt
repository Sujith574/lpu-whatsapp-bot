FEE & FINANCE RULES (2025–26)
====================================

LATE FEE:
• Approx ₹100/day (varies by university notice).

NON-PAYMENT:
• No exam admit card.
• Library access blocked.
• UMS services restricted.

FEE RECEIPTS:
• Available under UMS → Accounts → Payments.

SCHOLARSHIPS:
• Based on:
  – JEE/Board Marks
  – LPUNEST Score
  – Sports/Defense quotas
• Scholarship reduces each year if CGPA drops below criteria.

REFUNDS:
• Refund as per LPU refund policy.
• Hostel fee refunded only if requested in refund-eligible window.

LOAN LETTER:
• Available through RMS (Finance category).

FINE TYPES:
• Library fine
• Late fee fine
• Hostel overstay fine
• Damage fine
