ATTENDANCE RULES – LOVELY PROFESSIONAL UNIVERSITY (LPU)

1. Minimum Requirement:
   • Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory in every course.
   • Attendance is calculated course-wise, NOT overall.

2. Below 75% = SOA (Shortage of Attendance):
   • If attendance falls below 75%, the student will be marked SOA.
   • Students with SOA are NOT allowed to appear for end-term examinations.

3. Medical Attendance:
   • Medical attendance is added ONLY after approval from Uni-Health Centre.
   • Students must visit the University Hospital for medical attendance.
   • External/local medical certificates are NOT considered unless verified.

4. Attendance Cut-off:
   • Attendance is counted only till the last teaching day (as per Academic Calendar).

5. Attendance Viewing:
   • Students can check attendance on UMS:
     UMS → Academics → Attendance

6. Attendance Issues:
   • Students facing discrepancies must raise RMS:
     UMS → RMS → Log Request → Attendance Category

7. What to do if your attendance is low (example: 60%):
   • Attend all remaining classes regularly.
   • Meet your faculty advisor for guidance.
   • Medical attendance may be added only if applicable.
   • No special relaxation is given below 75% unless approved by higher authorities.

8. Important Notes:
   • Attending non-academic events without permission may reduce attendance.
   • Proxy attendance is a serious offense & handled as a disciplinary case.
