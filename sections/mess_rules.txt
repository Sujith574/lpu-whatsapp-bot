LPU MESS RULES & GUIDELINES (2025–26)
Division of Residential Services  
Official Students’ Mess Policy

=====================================================

=====================================================

-------------------------------

---------------------------------
B) SUNDAYS & HOLIDAYS
---------------------------------
Breakfast : 08:00 AM – 10:00 AM  
Lunch     : 12:30 PM – 03:00 PM  
Tea/Snack : 04:45 PM – 06:00 PM  
Dinner    : 07:30 PM – 09:30 PM  

=====================================================
2. ENTRY RULES
=====================================================
✔ Students must carry their **mobile phone OR ID card** for mess QR/validation.  
✔ Entry will NOT be allowed without verification.  
✔ Students must be in **decent dress** (no nightwear, no improper clothes).

=====================================================
3. MESS BEHAVIOUR RULES
=====================================================
Strictly prohibited inside the mess:

❌ Fighting, shouting, arguing  
❌ Abusing staff or creating disturbance  
❌ Wasting food intentionally  
❌ Taking utensils outside the mess  
❌ Throwing food or creating a mess around tables  
❌ Recording or making videos of staff without permission  

Allowed:
✔ Eat peacefully  
✔ Maintain decorum  
✔ Cooperate with mess staff  
✔ Inform warden/mess in-charge if issues occur  

If any issue occurs:
→ Contact Mess In-Charge  
→ Contact Hostel Warden  
→ Raise RMS complaint (category: Mess Issues)

=====================================================
4. FOOD SERVICE RULES
=====================================================
• Food will NOT be served in rooms.  
• Exception: **Sick students** (only with warden approval).  
• Menu changes are notified by Residential Services.  
• Students should not enter the kitchen area.  
• Mess staff are not allowed to take orders directly from students.

=====================================================
5. SICK FOOD POLICY
=====================================================
If a student is ill:
✔ They must inform the hostel warden.  
✔ Warden will authorize “Sick Food” delivery to room.  
✔ Food options may include:
  - Khichdi  
  - Dalia  
  - Simple roti/sabji  
  - Curd  

Sick food is given only after warden approval.

=====================================================
6. DISCIPLINE & ACTIONS
=====================================================
Violations may lead to:
• Warning  
• Suspension from mess for a period  
• University disciplinary action  
• Fine (depending on severity)

Examples:
❌ Fighting → Strict disciplinary action  
❌ Damaging mess property → Fine + action  
❌ Abusing staff → UMC/disciplinary case  

=====================================================
7. HYGIENE & CLEANLINESS
=====================================================
Students must:
✔ Dispose waste in designated bins  
✔ Keep tables clean after eating  
✔ Avoid spilling food  
✔ Maintain personal hygiene inside mess  

=====================================================
8. EXTRA INFORMATION STUDENTS OFTEN ASK
=====================================================

Q: *“Can I pack food from mess?”*  
A: Packing is usually **not allowed**, except sick food approved by warden.

Q: *“Can I take my parents to the mess?”*  
A: Depends on hostel. Usually **parents are not allowed** inside mess.

Q: *“Can I enter the mess after timings?”*  
A: No. Mess closes exactly at the prescribed timings.

Q: *“Is mess mandatory for hostellers?”*  
A: Yes. Every hostel resident must register for mess service.

Q: *“Where to complain about mess quality?”*  
A: UMS → RMS → Mess Category → Complaint.
