SECURITY RULES (2025–26)
==============================

RFID ENTRY:
• Students must carry ID card at all times.

GATE ENTRY:
• Biometric verification mandatory.
• Random checks possible.

VEHICLES:
• Only registered vehicles allowed.
• Speed limit: 30 km/hr.

CCTV:
• Campus under 24×7 surveillance.

REPORT ISSUES:
• Lost ID, safety concerns, theft → Report instantly via RMS (Security Category).
