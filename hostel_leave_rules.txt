LPU HOSTEL LEAVE RULES  
(Official Residential Handbook – 2025–26)

-------------------------------------------------------
1. LEAVE APPLICATION PATH (IMPORTANT)
-------------------------------------------------------
Students MUST apply for leave ONLY through:

UMS → Residential Services → Hostel Related Services → Apply Hostel Leave

No verbal or informal leave is accepted.

-------------------------------------------------------
2. TYPES OF HOSTEL LEAVES & ENTITLEMENTS
-------------------------------------------------------

A) DAY LEAVE  
Purpose: Short time movement outside the university campus  
Entitlement:
• Diploma Students: 40 per term  
• UG/PG Students: 75 per term  
• PhD Students: 150 per term  

B) NIGHT LEAVE  
Purpose: Staying outside overnight (travel, home visit, etc.)  
Entitlement:
• 60 leaves in Term I (July–Dec)  
• 60 leaves in Term II (Jan–May)  
Return Rule:
→ Must return only after **5:00 AM** of next day and before hostel attendance.

C) EXTENDED LEAVE (Day Extended / Night Extended)  
Purpose: To extend an existing leave by *maximum 3 hours*.  
Entitlement:
• 30 per category per term (30 Day Extended + 30 Night Extended)

Note:
→ Students can use Extended Leave only **8 times per month** (combined).

D) EMERGENCY LEAVE  
Purpose: Medical or other emergencies after attendance time.  
Rules:
• Can be applied **only by the warden**  
• Requires parent consent  
• Depends on emergency situation  
Entitlement:
• 4 per term (July–Dec)  
• 4 per term (Jan–May)

E) EXTENDED LEAVE (WITHIN CAMPUS)  
Special facility for female students.  
• They may step out of hostel AFTER attendance time.  
• MUST return within **3 hours** after attendance start time.  
• Only designated campus areas allowed.  
Male students:
• Movement allowed only in designated areas  
• Must return within 3 hours.

-------------------------------------------------------
3. LEAVE TIMINGS
-------------------------------------------------------
• Students may go on leave starting from **05:00 AM** each day.  
• Return must align with:
  – Leave category  
  – Attendance timings  
  – Maximum allowed hours

-------------------------------------------------------
4. LEAVE CONVERSION RULES
-------------------------------------------------------
A student may convert one type of leave to another **before check-in**, depending on circumstances.

-------------------------
A) From DAY LEAVE  
Can convert to:
• Day Extended Leave  
• Night Leave  
• Night Extended Leave  

-------------------------
B) From DAY EXTENDED LEAVE  
Upgrade:
• Night Leave  
• Night Extended Leave  
Downgrade:
• Day Leave (allowed ONLY up to attendance start time)

-------------------------
C) From NIGHT LEAVE  
Downgrade:
• Day Extended Leave  
• Day Leave  
Upgrade:
• Night Extended Leave  
• Multi-day Night Leave  

-------------------------
D) From NIGHT EXTENDED LEAVE  
Downgrade:
• Day Extended Leave  
• Day Leave  
• Night Leave  
Upgrade:
• Multi-day Night Leave or Night Extended Leave  

Notes:
• All conversions MUST be done before Check-In.  
• Leave balance must be available.  
• Attendance and timings must not be violated.

-------------------------------------------------------
5. LEAVE ENTITLEMENT EXTENSION
-------------------------------------------------------
Students may request additional leave days (max **10 days**) ONLY IF:
• All leave quota is exhausted AND  
• Valid reasons exist (Medical treatment, Placement drives, OJT, Emergencies)

Approval requires:
• Proper documents  
• Parent consent  
• Verification by authorities  

-------------------------------------------------------
6. PARENT NOTIFICATION SYSTEM
-------------------------------------------------------
Parents receive updates via official channels for:
• Leave Application  
• Check-Out  
• Check-In  
• Leave violations (late entry, overstay)

-------------------------------------------------------
7. CHECK-OUT & CHECK-IN RULES
-------------------------------------------------------
CHECK-OUT:  
• Leave slip is generated in LPU Touch  
• Security verifies leave slip at the main gate

CHECK-IN:  
• Student must show leave slip at entry gate  
• Then MUST meet hostel warden before attendance time  

-------------------------------------------------------
8. IMPORTANT GENERAL GUIDELINES
-------------------------------------------------------
• Students must ensure **academic attendance is not affected** when taking leave.  
• Authorities may change leave entitlements anytime for security reasons.  
• Failure to follow check-in/check-out rules may result in disciplinary action.  
• Students cannot bypass hostel attendance through leave misusage.

LPU HOSTEL LEAVE RULES & GUIDELINES (2025–26)
Official Residential Services Policy
==============================================================

==============================================================
1. LEAVE APPLICATION PATH (VERY IMPORTANT)
==============================================================
UMS → Residential Services → Hostel Related Services → Apply Hostel Leave

All hostel leaves must be applied through UMS only.

Parent will receive SMS for:
• Leave Applied
• Leave Approved
• Check-out
• Check-in

Warden approves all leave requests.

==============================================================
2. TYPES OF LEAVE & ENTITLEMENT
==============================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------
A) DAY LEAVE (Short outdoor visit)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose:
• Market work
• Personal work
• Coaching
• Shopping
• Spending few hours outside campus

Entitlement per academic term:
• Diploma: 40 Day Leaves
• UG/PG: 75 Day Leaves
• PhD: 150 Day Leaves

Check-out time: After 05:00 AM  
Must return before: Hostel Attendance Time

--------------------------------------------------------------
B) NIGHT LEAVE (Stay outside campus at night)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose:
• Going home
• Traveling
• Staying overnight outside

Entitlement:
• 60 Night Leaves per term (July–Dec)
• 60 Night Leaves per term (Jan–May)

Return on next day ONLY after **5:00 AM** and before hostel attendance.

--------------------------------------------------------------
C) EXTENDED LEAVE (Day Extended / Night Extended)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose:
• Delay in reaching campus due to unavoidable situations  
• Allows extra 3 hours beyond regular leave

Entitlement:
• 30 Day Extended per term
• 30 Night Extended per term  

Max usage: 8 times per month.

--------------------------------------------------------------
D) EMERGENCY LEAVE (Only by warden)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Used for:
• Medical emergency
• Sudden family emergency
• Special urgent cases

Important:
• Warden applies this on behalf of student  
• Parent consent is mandatory  
• Limited to 4 per term

--------------------------------------------------------------
E) EXTENDED LEAVE (Within Campus)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Special leave for **Female Students** after attendance.

Allowed Areas:
• Academic Blocks
• Indoor facilities
• Food courts
• Auditoriums

Must return within **3 hours** of attendance time.

Males can roam only in designated male-friendly zones.

==============================================================
3. HOSTEL ATTENDANCE TIMINGS
==============================================================
These are STRICT timings:

• 01 May – 31 Oct → **8:00 PM**  
• 01 Nov – 28 Feb → **7:00 PM**  
• 01 Mar – 30 Apr → **7:30 PM**

If a student is not present → Marked **ABSENT** (disciplinary action).

==============================================================
4. LATE NIGHT ENTRY
==============================================================
If student returns AFTER attendance time:

→ Marked as **Late Night Entry**  
→ Security records it  
→ Warden may take disciplinary action  
→ Parent may receive alert  

==============================================================
5. OVERSTAY (VERY STRICT RULE)
==============================================================
If student does NOT return on time:

→ Marked as **Overstay**  
→ Must immediately extend leave via UMS  
→ Student must inform warden  
→ Disciplinary action may apply  
→ Only allowed if genuine reason with proof

==============================================================
6. LEAVE CONVERSION (UPGRADE / DOWNGRADE)
==============================================================

IMPORTANT:
Leave can be converted ONLY **before check-in**.

--------------------------------------------------------------
A) Convert Day Leave →
--------------------------------------------------------------
Upgrade:
• Day Extended Leave  
• Night Leave  
• Night Extended Leave  

--------------------------------------------------------------
B) Convert Day Extended Leave →
--------------------------------------------------------------
Upgrade:
• Night Leave  
• Night Extended Leave  

Downgrade:
• Back to Day Leave  
(Max return by attendance time)

--------------------------------------------------------------
C) Convert Night Leave →
--------------------------------------------------------------
Downgrade:
• Day Extended Leave  
• Day Leave  
(Max return by attendance time next day)

Upgrade:
• Night Extended Leave  
• Extend to multi-day night leave

--------------------------------------------------------------
D) Convert Night Extended Leave →
--------------------------------------------------------------
Downgrade:
• Day Extended Leave  
• Day Leave  
• Night Leave  

Upgrade:
• Multi-day night / night-extended leave

==============================================================
7. CHECK-OUT PROCESS
==============================================================
At main gate:
1. Show approval leave slip on LPU Touch / UMS  
2. Security scans & verifies  
3. Parent gets SMS alert  
4. Student exits campus

==============================================================
8. CHECK-IN PROCESS
==============================================================
At main gate:
1. Show leave slip  
2. Security verifies  
3. Leave is officially closed  
4. Parent gets check-in SMS  
5. Student must report to warden before attendance time

==============================================================

==============================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------

B) Leave entitlement extension
--------------------------------------------------------------
Allowed only for:
• Medical emergency

• Internship  
• Special cases (with documents)

--------------------------------------------------------------
C) Parents are always informed
--------------------------------------------------------------
Every leave is:
• Logged  
• Time-stamped  
• Sent as SMS to parents  

--------------------------------------------------------------
D) Hostel Stay Back (During Class Hours)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Allowed only if:
• Medical issue (doctor note required)  
• Special permission from authority  

Parents will be informed.
