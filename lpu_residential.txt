LPU HOSTEL RESIDENTIAL HANDBOOK (2025–26) — STUDENT VERSION

1. INTRODUCTION
- Hostels provide bed, mattress, table, chair, almirah, AC/cooler (as per room), geyser, Wi-Fi, 24h power backup, mess, gym, hospital & ambulance.

2. HOSTEL INDUCTION
- Students must attend induction to learn rules, safety, and facilities.
- Essentials: bed sheet, pillow, blanket, bucket, mug, towel, toiletries, footwear.
- Prohibited items: heaters, immersion rods, electrical cookers, mixers, ovens (except allowed apartment induction).

3. HOSTEL ROOM CHANGE & MUTUAL SHIFT
- UMS → Residential Services → Residential Facilities Booking → Hostel Booking / Mutual Shifting.
- Upgrade: pay difference. Downgrade: allowed before reporting.
- Mutual shifting: ₹1000 each participant.

4. HOSTEL TIMINGS & MESS
- Mess (Working days): Breakfast 07:15–09:30 | Lunch 11:30–15:00 | Snacks 16:45–18:00 | Dinner 19:30–21:30
- Silence/Study hours: 22:00 – 06:00
- Hostels attendance timings: 1 May–31 Oct: 20:00 | 1 Nov–28 Feb: 19:00 | 1 Mar–30 Apr: 19:30

5. HOSTEL LEAVE
- UMS → Residential Services → Apply Hostel Leave
- Day Leave: Diploma 40, UG/PG 75, PhD 150 per term
- Night Leave: 60 per term (each term)
- Extended Leave (±3 hrs): 30 per term
- Emergency Leave: 4 per term (warden only)
- Leave conversion allowed before check-in.
- Leave extension (medical/OJT) up to 10 days (approval required).

6. ANTI-RAGGING
- Ragging strictly prohibited. Report to Anti-Ragging helpline: 01824-444097 / 9876644331.

7. SECURITY & TURNSTILES
- ID & biometric required at turnstiles.
- Lost ID: temporary pass for 2 days; apply for new ID next day at Block 3 Room 104.
- CCTV 24×7; gates monitored.

8. CODE OF CONDUCT
Do's:
- Wear ID; follow timings; maintain hygiene; report suspicious activity.

Don'ts:
- No drugs, alcohol, smoking.
- No trespass into other hostels.
- No unauthorized gatherings.
- No tampering with university property.

9. RMS (Request Management System)
- UMS → RMS → Log Request
- Categories: Hostel allotment, maintenance, staff behavior, fees.
- Resolution timeline: 2–3 working days.

10. DE-INDUCTION & CLOAK ROOM
- Cloak room available via UMS. Items allowed: blanket (1), mug (1), bucket (1), bags (2).
- Return: OTP authentication; late pickup fine ₹100/day.

11. EMERGENCY (Medical / Fire / Lift)
- Medical help: Uni Health Center (24×7).
- Fire: Inform warden; use stairs; stay low; cover mouth; don't use lift.
- Lift emergency: push alarm; call numbers inside lift.

12. IMPORTANT CONTACTS
- Warden offices, Residential Services, Helpdesks and emergency numbers are maintained in security file and main knowledge base.
