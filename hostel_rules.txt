LPU HOSTEL RULES & REGULATIONS (2025–26)
============================================

ENTRY TIMINGS:
• Girls Hostel: 10:00 PM 
• Boys Hostel: 11:00 PM
• Attendance marked physically by warden every day.

ABSENT / LATE ENTRY:
• If student is not present during hostel attendance ⇒ marked Absent.
• Late entry after permissible time ⇒ strict disciplinary action.

SILENCE HOURS:
• 10:00 PM – 06:00 AM strict silence.
• No roaming allowed after 10:00 PM.

ROOM RESPONSIBILITY:
• Students must keep rooms clean.
• Damages → student pays damage charges.
• Cooking/heating appliances are NOT allowed.

VISITORS:
• Allowed only in visitor rooms.
• Only parents/guardians approved in UMS can visit.
• Timings:  
  – Working days: 4 PM–7 PM  
  – Holidays: 9 AM–7 PM

PROHIBITED ITEMS:
• Heaters, induction plates, electric cookers, kettles (standard rooms)
• Irons, immersion rods
• Alcohol, cigarettes, drugs
• Weapons, harmful objects

ANTI-RAGGING:
• Zero tolerance.
• Report immediately to Anti-Ragging Helpline: 9876644331 / 01824-444097

MESS:
• Food will not be served in rooms except sick cases with warden approval.

HOSTEL STAYBACK:
• Staying in hostel during class hours requires medical/special permission.
