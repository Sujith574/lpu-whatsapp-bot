LPU RFID, VEHICLE ENTRY, PARKING & TRAFFIC RULES
(Official Security & Safety Guidelines – 2025–26)

=====================================================
1. OVERVIEW
=====================================================
LPU regulates entry of all vehicles using:
• RFID-based boom barrier system
• CCTV monitoring at all lanes
• Automated entry/exit logs
• Strict parking zones for students, staff, and visitors

Vehicles without RFID are NOT allowed beyond Gate 1-A without special approval.

=====================================================
2. TYPES OF VEHICLES ALLOWED
=====================================================
✔ Allowed:
• Two-wheelers (bike/scooter)
• Four-wheelers (cars)
• Official vehicles
• Registered vendor vehicles

❌ NOT ALLOWED:
• Open Jeeps  
• Vehicles with dark/black tinted glasses  
• Modified exhaust vehicles (loud silencers)  
• Unauthorized commercial vehicles  

=====================================================
3. RFID SYSTEM (Radio Frequency Identification)
=====================================================
RFID is mandatory for:
• Hostel students (2W / 4W)
• Day scholars (where applicable)
• Staff & faculty vehicles
• Vendors/contractors using regular entry

RFID allows automatic entry/exit through boom barriers.

=====================================================
4. HOW TO APPLY FOR RFID
=====================================================
UMS Path:
UMS → Division of Security & Safety → RFID → RFID Online Application Form

You must upload:
• Driving License (soft copy)
• RC (Registration Certificate)
• Vehicle Insurance
• Fee receipt (if applicable)

RFID is issued at:
Block 29 → Room 307  
(Security & Safety Division)

=====================================================
5. STUDENT RFID VALIDITY & RULES
=====================================================
• RFID for students is valid for ONE academic year  
  (1st August – 31st May).  
• Automatically deactivated on 31 May every year.  
• Hostellers can apply for free/paid RFID as per policy.

IMPORTANT:
→ RFID MUST be pasted on the windshield  
→ Holding RFID in hand = **disciplinary action**  
→ RFID is linked to the STUDENT, not the vehicle user  

No student can use another student’s RFID.

=====================================================
6. LOSS OR DAMAGE OF RFID
=====================================================
If RFID is lost/damaged:
• Report immediately to Main Security Office  
• Old RFID will be deactivated  
• Apply for a new RFID  

Faulty RFID repair:
Block 30 → Room 303

Activation/deactivation issues:
Block 31 → Room 104

=====================================================
7. VEHICLE ENTRY PROCESS (Boom Barriers)
=====================================================
Boom Barriers are at Gate 1-A (11 lanes).  
Functionality:
• Automatically open when RFID is detected  
• Lane cameras record vehicle number and entry time  
• Visitor vehicles are manually approved by guard

Students must:
• Slow down  
• Keep RFID clearly visible  
• Avoid tailgating another vehicle  

=====================================================
8. SPEED LIMITS & TRAFFIC RULES
=====================================================
Speed Limit INSIDE Campus → **30 km/hr**

STRICTLY PROHIBITED:
❌ Driving without helmet (rider + pillion)  
❌ Triple riding  
❌ Stunt riding  
❌ Honking unnecessarily  
❌ Riding in reverse direction  
❌ Using headphones/earphones while driving  

Morning One-Way Rule:
• From 8:40 AM – 9:00 AM all internal roads become ONE-WAY for incoming traffic.

=====================================================
9. PARKING AREAS
=====================================================
Parking is allowed ONLY in designated zones.

-------------------------------------------
A) DAY SCHOLAR PARKING (7 AM – 7 PM)
-------------------------------------------
1. Parking P–1 → Main Gate 1-A  
   For: Students with classes in Blocks 1–39  
   Vehicle: 2W/4W  

2. Parking P–27 → Southern Gate  
   For: Blocks 55–58 students  
   Note: Vehicles can be taken out **after 11 AM only**

-------------------------------------------
B) HOSTEL PARKING (24×7)
-------------------------------------------
1. GH/BH specific parking zones  
2. Students must park ONLY in their hostel’s allotted area  
3. First row in some areas reserved for:
   • Security officials  
   • Executive Deans  
   • Visitors  

-------------------------------------------
C) TEMPORARY PARKING
-------------------------------------------
Issued for:
• Medical conditions  
• Short-term parking near academic blocks  
Permission needed from **Main Security Office**

=====================================================
10. HOSTEL STUDENT VEHICLE RULES
=====================================================
Hostellers can use vehicles **only after university hours**.

Allowed vehicle usage:
✔ 5 PM – 8 PM  
✔ On holidays/Sundays → 7 AM – 7 PM  

NOT ALLOWED:
❌ Using vehicle during class hours  
❌ Using another student’s vehicle  
❌ Driving without helmet  
❌ Using open jeeps or cars with dark glasses  

Hostellers MUST apply for RFID before bringing vehicle inside.

=====================================================
11. PARKING AT OWNER’S RISK
=====================================================
University is NOT responsible for:
• Theft  
• Loss  
• Damage  
• Accessories loss  

Owners must lock vehicles properly.

=====================================================
12. DOCUMENTS CHECKED OFTEN BY SECURITY
=====================================================
Students must carry:
• RFID  
• Driving License  
• RC  
• Insurance  
• Pollution Certificate  

Failure = fine + disciplinary action.
