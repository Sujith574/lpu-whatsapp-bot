LPU SECURITY & ENTRY/EXIT — SUMMARY

MAIN GATE 1-A
- Location: On NH-1 (after Chaheru railway bridge coming from Delhi/Ludhiana).
- 24×7 entry/exit for pedestrians and vehicles.
- 11 electronic boom barriers for registered vehicles (RFID).
- Reception center issues visitor passes (after approval).
- Turnstile gates and security staff manage pedestrian entry.
- Second turnstile near student parking for day scholars.

MAIN GATE 1-B
- Pedestrian gate; biometric authentication.
- 24×7 pedestrian entry/exit.
- Frisking room, CCTV, PA system, IP phones, security office.
- Alternate vehicle gate for emergencies.
- Dedicated turnstile room for hostel student leave check-in/out.

VISITOR PASS MANAGEMENT
- Types of visits: Scheduled (pre-approved) and Unscheduled.
- Online Sponsored Parent Pass: Generated via UMS (Student / Staff / Faculty).
- Manual visitor pass: Issued at reception for unscheduled visitors (one day only).
- Visitor exit requires signatures and bar-code scan at gate.

TURNSTILE GATES
- Present at Main Gates, hostels, library, auditorium, staff residences.
- Biometric/card scan mandatory.
- Managed 24×7 by security staff; CCTV monitored.
- Lost UID → temporary pass for 1 day; new UID issued from Block 3, Room 104.

TRAFFIC & PARKING
- Allowed: two-wheelers, four-wheelers.
- Forbidden: open jeeps, vehicles with dark/black tinted glasses.
- Speed limit: 30 km/hr.
- Pedestrians must use paver footpaths and zebra crossings.
- Day Parking: designated areas (P-1, P-27 etc.).
- Hostel Parking: reserved (P-8, P-12, P-14, etc.).
- Temporary parking: only with written approval.

HOSTEL STUDENT VEHICLE GUIDELINES
- Vehicles allowed after university hours; allocated parking near hostel.
- Apply for RFID before bringing vehicle inside campus.
- One RFID per vehicle; pasted on windshield.
- Helmets mandatory for rider & pillion.
- No triple riding or stunts; silencer noise must be within limits.
- University not responsible for theft/damage.

RFID & BOOM BARRIERS
- RFID linked to RC, DL, insurance; used to auto-open boom barriers.
- RFID issued from Division of Security & Safety (Block 29 Room 307).
- Activation issues → Block 31 Room 104. Faulty RFID → Block 30 Room 303.
- For lost RFID report immediately to security for deactivation.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR RFID
- Driving License (soft copy)
- Vehicle RC (soft copy)
- Insurance copy (soft copy)
- Fee receipt (if applicable)
