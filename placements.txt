PLACEMENTS & TRAINING (2025–26)
======================================

ELIGIBILITY:
• Minimum CGPA required (company specific)
• No active backlogs for most companies
• Attendance > 75%
• Good discipline record

PRE-PLACEMENT:
• Attend placement classes
• Aptitude & coding tests
• Mock interviews

PLACEMENT PROCESS:
• Registration → Shortlisting → Test → Interview → Offer Letter

INTERNSHIP:
• Some companies offer PPO after internship.

SUPPORT:
• Division of Career Services (DCS)
• Practice portal, mock tests, profile building support

PLACEMENT BLOCK:
• Misbehavior or poor attendance may block placement access.
