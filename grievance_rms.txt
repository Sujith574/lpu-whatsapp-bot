RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (RMS)
Lovely Professional University (2025–26)
==============================================================

The Relationship Management System (RMS) is the official platform for submitting complaints, 
service requests, and queries. All issues must be raised through RMS so that they are tracked, 
documented, and resolved on time.

UMS Path:
UMS → Relationship Management System (RMS) → Log Request

==============================================================
1. PURPOSE OF RMS
==============================================================

RMS ensures:
• Fast resolution of student issues  
• Proper tracking and monitoring  
• Transparent actions taken  
• Accountability from departments  
• Automatic notifications to students and parents  

Students must NOT raise complaints through WhatsApp, email, or phone unless it is an emergency.  
All official issues must be submitted only through RMS.

==============================================================

==============================================================
3. RMS RESOLUTION TIMELINES
==============================================================

Faculty / Warden / Department:
• General issues → 2–3 working days  
• High priority → Same day  
• Technical (UMS/exam/marks) → 3–7 working days  

Maintenance:
• Emergency (water/electricity) → 0–4 hours  
• AC/Plumbing/Carpentry → 1–3 days  
• Lift/WiFi → 24–72 hours  

Departments update the timelines when needed.

==============================================================
4. TRACKING YOUR RMS REQUEST
==============================================================

UMS Path:
UMS → RMS → My Requests

Statuses:
• Open – Submitted  
• In Process – Being worked on  
• Escalated – Sent to higher authority  
• Resolved – Issue fixed  
• Closed – Closed by department/student  

Students must choose: “Satisfied / Not Satisfied”

==============================================================
5. ESCALATION PROCESS
==============================================================

Level 1 → Assigned staff/faculty  
Level 2 → Warden/Department  
Level 3 → HOD  
Level 4 → Division Head / Dean  
Level 5 → Registrar (rare cases)

Manual escalation:
• Add a comment  
• Select “Not satisfied with response”

==============================================================
6. DOCUMENTS REQUIRED (IF APPLICABLE)
==============================================================

• Screenshot  
• Photo/video of issue  
• Fee receipt  
• Attendance proof  
• Medical documents  

==============================================================
7. RULES WHILE RAISING RMS
==============================================================

Students must:
• Use respectful language  
• Explain issue clearly  
• Attach evidence  
• Avoid abusive words  

Violations → Ticket closed or disciplinary action.

==============================================================

==============================================================

Attendance:  
“My attendance on 10 Feb is absent but I was present. Kindly check.”

Mess:  
“Food quality in BH-5 lunch today was poor. Please review.”

Maintenance:  
“Light not working in BH-4 C-212.”

Academic:  
“My internal marks for CSE204 are missing.”

Hostel:  
“Mutual shifting option not visible on UMS.”

==============================================================
9. WHEN NOT TO USE RMS
==============================================================

Do NOT use RMS for:
• Fights between students  
• Emergency medical cases  
• Ragging/harassment (use Anti-Ragging Helpline)  
• Personal disputes  

==============================================================
10. RMS FOR HOSTEL LEAVE ISSUES
==============================================================

Use RMS if:
• Leave not approved  
• Wrong leave marked  
• Check-in missing  
• Parent OTP not received  
• Day leave count mismatch
