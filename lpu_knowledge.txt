LPU OFFICIAL KNOWLEDGE DATABASE

ATTENDANCE RULES:
- Minimum 75% attendance required in each course.
- Below 75% = SOA (Shortage of Attendance).
- Students with SOA are not allowed to write exams unless special permission is granted.
- Medical attendance is added only after proper medical approval.
- Attendance is counted until the last teaching day.

HOSTEL TIMINGS:
- Girls Hostel In-time: 10:00 PM.
- Boys Hostel In-time: 11:00 PM.
- Leave Pass is required for hostel exit.
- Night-out requires parent/guardian consent + warden approval.
- leave apply path: ums > ums navigation > administrative > residential services > hostel leave application. 

REAPPEAR RULES:
- Reappear exam fee is ₹500 per course.
- Internal marks remain the same.
- Only end-term marks are replaced after reappear.
- Missing a reappear exam results in no second chance.

CGPA RULES:
- CGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) / ΣCredits.
- Grade Points: O=10, A+=9, A=8, B+=7, B=6, C=5, D=4.

EXAM RULES:
- University ID card + admit card are mandatory.
- Late entry beyond 15 minutes is not allowed.
- Mobile phones are strictly prohibited in exam halls.
- Any form of cheating results in a UMC (University Management Case).

DISCIPLINE:
- Ragging is strictly prohibited.
- Consumption or possession of drugs/alcohol is banned.
- Non-veg food is not allowed inside the university campus.
- Fighting, bullying, harassment, abusive behavior, or misuse of social media is prohibited.

DRESS CODE:
- Formal uniform is mandatory from Monday to Friday.
- Casual wear is allowed on Saturday and Sunday.

FEE RULES:
- Late fine is approximately ₹100 per day (may vary).
- Students with pending fees will not receive exam admit cards.

LEAVE RULES:
- Leave must be applied through UMS.
- All leaves require warden + department approval.

MEDICAL:
- Students must visit the university hospital for medical attendance.
- Medical certificates are considered only after approval from the medical center.

LIBRARY:
- Silence must be maintained inside the library.
- Late return of books is strictly prohibited and may lead to disciplinary action or fines.

TRANSPORT:
- Bus pass is mandatory for boarding university buses.
- Any misconduct inside the bus results in strict disciplinary action.

GRIEVANCE:
- Students can submit grievances via:
  UMS → Student Services → Relationship Management System (RMS) → Grievance.
- student having any issues regarding anything then he/she can log an complaint through rms. 

PLACEMENTS:
- Minimum CGPA requirement varies based on company.

- Students must attend placement classes, pre-placement talks, and tests.
