LPU OFFICIAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

────────────────────────────────────────────
ATTENDANCE RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────
• Minimum 75% attendance required per course.
• Below 75% = SOA → not allowed for end-term exams.
• Medical attendance counted only when taken from Uni-Health Centre.
• Attendance check: UMS → Academics → Attendance.
• Attendance disputes → Raise RMS (Attendance Category).
• Low attendance? Attend all remaining classes, meet faculty, apply medical if valid.

────────────────────────────────────────────
CGPA & GRADING
────────────────────────────────────────────
• CGPA = Σ (Credit × Grade Point) / Total Credits.
• Grade Points:
  O = 10
  A+ = 9
  A = 8
  B+ = 7
  B = 6
  C = 5
  D = 4
• Reappear improves only end-term marks, not internals.
• CGPA improves only if your grade improves.
• Transcripts available via UMS or Registrar Office.

────────────────────────────────────────────
DISCIPLINE RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────
• Ragging, fights, alcohol, smoking, abusive behavior → strict action.
• Cheating during exams → UMC (University Management Case).
• Social media abuse → Cyber Cell action + disciplinary action.
• Possible actions: Warning, Fine, Suspension, Rustication.

────────────────────────────────────────────
DRESS CODE
────────────────────────────────────────────
• Formal uniform mandatory: Monday–Friday.
• Casual wear allowed: Saturday–Sunday.
• No shorts, ripped jeans, or slippers allowed in academic blocks.
• Lab coat mandatory during labs/workshops.

────────────────────────────────────────────
EXAM & REAPPEAR RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────
• Reappear exam fee: ₹500 per course.
• Only end-term marks are replaced; internal marks remain same.
• ID Card + Admit Card mandatory.
• No phones, earphones, smartwatch, digital devices allowed.
• Late entry after 15 minutes is not allowed.
• UMC → exam cancellation + disciplinary action.

────────────────────────────────────────────
EXAM HALL RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────
• Report 15 minutes early.
• Only non-programmable calculator allowed.
• Rough work only on sheets provided.
• No leaving hall in first or last 30 minutes.

────────────────────────────────────────────
FEE & ACCOUNTS
────────────────────────────────────────────
• Late fee approx ₹100 per day (varies).
• Non-payment may block UMS or prevent exam entry.
• Fee receipts: UMS → Accounts.
• Scholarships: JEE/Boards/LPUNEST/Sports/Defence.
• Refund as per LPU Refund Policy.

────────────────────────────────────────────
RMS SYSTEM (COMPLAINTS)
────────────────────────────────────────────
• All issues must be logged through RMS.
• Path: UMS → RMS → Log Request.
• Categories include: Attendance, Marks, Hostel, Mess, Maintenance, WiFi, Electricity.
• Usual resolution time: 2–3 working days.
• Escalation chain: Warden → HOD → Division Head.

────────────────────────────────────────────
HOSTEL ATTENDANCE & TIMINGS
────────────────────────────────────────────
Attendance timings depend on season:
• May–October: 8 PM
• November–February: 7 PM
• March–April: 7:30 PM

Rules:
• Late entry recorded and may lead to strict action.
• Students must return before hostel attendance time daily.

────────────────────────────────────────────
HOSTEL LEAVE RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────
• All leave must be applied via UMS.
• Day Leave: 75 per term (UG/PG).
• Night Leave: 60 per term.
• Extended Leave: +3 hours (30 per term).
• Emergency Leave: granted by warden only.
• Return after night leave: after 5 AM but before attendance.
• Overstay → disciplinary action.

────────────────────────────────────────────
HOSTEL RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────
• Silence hours: 10 PM – 6 AM.
• No roaming after 10 PM.
• Cooking appliances (stoves, heaters, induction) are banned.
• Visitors allowed only in visitor rooms.
• Ragging, bullying, harassment → zero tolerance.

────────────────────────────────────────────
HOSTEL QUICK TIMING INFO
────────────────────────────────────────────
• Return before attendance time.
• After attendance → no movement (except approved extended leave for girls hostel).

────────────────────────────────────────────
ID CARD RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────
• ID card mandatory everywhere (hostel, mess, exam, library, blocks).

• Using another student’s ID → strict disciplinary action.

LIBRARY RULES
