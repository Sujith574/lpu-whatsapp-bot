ATTENDANCE RULES
• Minimum 75% required per course.
• Below 75% = SOA → not allowed for end-term exam.
• Medical attendance only via Uni-Health Centre.
• Check attendance: UMS → Academics → Attendance.
• Issue with attendance → Raise RMS (Attendance Category).
• Low attendance what to do? Attend all remaining classes, meet faculty, apply medical if valid.

CGPA & GRADING
• CGPA = (Credit × Grade Point) / Total Credits.
• Grade Points: O-10, A+-9, A-8, B+-7, B-6, C-5, D-4.
• Reappear improves only end-term marks.
• CGPA improves only if course grade improves.
• Transcripts: UMS or Registrar office.

DISCIPLINE RULES
• Ragging, fights, alcohol, smoking, abuse = strict action.
• Cheating in exams = UMC case.
• Social media abuse → Cyber Cell action.
• Actions: Warning, fine, suspension, rustication.

DRESS CODE
• Formal uniform Mon–Fri.
• Casuals only Sat–Sun.
• No shorts, ripped jeans, slippers in academics.
• Lab coat mandatory in workshops/labs.

EXAM & REAPPEAR RULES
• Reappear fee ₹500 per course.
• Only end-term marks replaced.
• ID + Admit Card mandatory.
• No phones/smartwatch allowed.
• Late entry after 15 mins not allowed.
• UMC = exam cancelled + disciplinary action.

EXAM HALL RULES
• Report 15 mins early.
• Non-programmable calculator only.
• Rough work only on given sheet.
• No leaving hall in first/last 30 mins.

FEE & ACCOUNTS
• Late fee ~₹100/day (varies).
• Non-payment → block of UMS/exams.
• Receipts: UMS → Accounts.
• Scholarships: JEE/Boards/LPUNEST/Sports/Defence.
• Refund as per LPU refund policy.

RMS SYSTEM
• Raise all complaints in RMS.
• Paths: UMS → RMS → Log Request.
• Issues: Attendance, marks, hostel, mess, maintenance, WiFi, electricity.
• Resolution: 2–3 working days (maintenance faster).
• Escalation: Warden → HOD → Division Head.
