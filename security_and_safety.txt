LPU SECURITY & SAFETY RULES (2025–26)
Official Division of Security & Safety
==============================================================

The security department maintains strict 24×7 protection of all students, staff, and university property.
These rules must be followed by every resident and day scholar.

==============================================================
1. ENTRY / EXIT RULES (MAIN GATE)
==============================================================

• Students must use their **LPU ID Card (RFID)** to enter or exit through turnstiles.
• Biometric authentication is mandatory.
• Students must carry ID card at all times inside campus.
• No student can exit campus without:
  - Approved Leave Slip (Hostel Residents)
  - Valid pass (Day scholars during restricted hours)
• Parents receive SMS when students check out or check in.
• Attempting to enter/exit using another student’s ID is strictly prohibited.

--------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPORARY PERMIT (If ID lost)
--------------------------------------------------------------
• Report lost ID to hostel warden immediately.
• Warden issues a **Temporary Gate Pass (valid 2 days)**.
• Student must apply for new ID immediately.

==============================================================
2. TURNSTILES & BIOMETRIC RULES
==============================================================

• Every entry and exit must be recorded through turnstiles.
• Jumping, forcing, or damaging turnstiles is a major security violation.
• Students must pass one-by-one; group passing is not allowed.
• Do not block sensors, swipe from distance, or tailgate.

Violations result in:
• Warning or fine  
• Blocking of gate access  
• Disciplinary case  

==============================================================
3. VISITOR / PARENT ENTRY RULES
==============================================================

Parents/Guardians can visit students only during these timings:
• **Weekdays:** 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM  
• **Sundays/Holidays:** 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM  

Parents must:
• Carry valid government ID
• Enter only through main gate
• Follow visitor issuance process

Student must apply:
UMS → Security & Safety → **Online Sponsored Parent Pass**

Visitors:
• Can meet student ONLY in **Visitor Room**
• Are **not permitted** inside hostel rooms
• Cannot stay overnight unless using paid Guest House (with prior booking)

==============================================================
4. UNAUTHORIZED ENTRY/EXIT (STRICT VIOLATION)
==============================================================

The following are strictly prohibited:
• Entering/exiting without ID card  
• Entering/exiting using someone else’s ID  
• Jumping gates or climbing boundaries  
• Using vehicle gates for pedestrian entry  
• Hiding in vehicles to leave campus  

Punishments:
• Immediate disciplinary action  
• Gate access suspension  
• Hostel/department complaint  
• Parent notification  

==============================================================
5. PARKING & VEHICLE RULES
==============================================================

-----------------------------------------------
WHO CAN BRING VEHICLES?
-----------------------------------------------
Only students with valid permits:
• Registered two-wheeler  
• Registered four-wheeler (only final-year students with approval)

-----------------------------------------------
WHERE TO PARK?
-----------------------------------------------
• Parking is allowed ONLY in designated parking areas.
• Parking outside marked zones = fine.

-----------------------------------------------
VEHICLE SPEED LIMITS
-----------------------------------------------
• **Maximum speed inside campus: 30 km/hr**
• Driving without license is strictly prohibited.
• Triple-riding on bike = violation.
• No honking near academic blocks and hostels.

-----------------------------------------------
VEHICLES NOT ALLOWED
-----------------------------------------------
• Modified bikes / loud exhaust  
• E-bikes without registration  
• Rental bikes not registered with LPU  
• Unregistered visiting vehicles  

==============================================================
6. SECURITY VIOLATIONS & DISCIPLINARY ACTION
==============================================================

The following actions are strictly banned:
• Fighting / Physical altercation  
• Harassment / Eve-teasing  
• Abusive behavior towards students/staff  
• Theft or possession of stolen items  
• Carrying weapons or sharp objects  
• Damage to university property  
• Misuse of fire safety equipment  
• Tampering with cameras or access systems  

Penalties include:
• Fines  
• Suspension  
• Hostel expulsion  
• Academic block restrictions  
• UMC (University Management Case)  

==============================================================
7. CCTV SURVEILLANCE
==============================================================

• Entire campus is monitored 24×7.
• Includes hostels, academic blocks, food courts, sports areas.
• Footage is used for investigation and disciplinary cases.
• Students must NOT tamper with or block cameras.

==============================================================
9. FIRE SAFETY & EMERGENCY EVACUATION
==============================================================

In case of fire:
• Stay calm and alert warden/security immediately.
• Do **NOT** use the lift during fire.
• Use fire exits only.
• Crawl low to avoid smoke.
• Cover mouth with wet cloth.
• Allow rescue team to lead evacuation.

Do NOT:
• Try to extinguish electrical fire with water.
• Block fire exit or staircase.
• Misuse fire extinguishers.

==============================================================
10. SAFETY RULES FOR FEMALE STUDENTS
==============================================================

• Special extended leave (within campus) allowed after attendance.
• Must return within 3 hours.
• Can move only in designated safe zones.
• Dedicated female security staff available 24×7.

==============================================================
11. VEHICLE CHECKING & DOCUMENT VERIFICATION
==============================================================

Security may check:
• RC  
• Driving license  
• Helmet  
• Parking sticker  
• Vehicle condition  

Unregistered vehicles may be seized.

==============================================================
12. GENERAL SAFETY GUIDELINES
==============================================================

• Always carry ID card inside campus.
• Report suspicious activity immediately.
• Follow instructions from security personnel.
• Keep emergency numbers saved.
• Do not move in restricted zones after dark.
