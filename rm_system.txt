LPU RMS — RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
(Official Hostel & Academic Complaint System – 2025–26)

-------------------------------------------------------
1. WHAT IS RMS?
-------------------------------------------------------
RMS (Relationship Management System) is LPU’s official platform where students can raise ANY type of complaint or request.

All hostel, academic, administrative, maintenance, and residential problems MUST be submitted only through RMS.

-------------------------------------------------------
2. RMS APPLICATION PATH
-------------------------------------------------------
UMS → Request Management System (RMS) → Log Request

Students must select:
• Category  
• Subcategory  
• Block/Location  
• Description of issue  
• Upload image (optional but recommended)

-------------------------------------------------------
3. TYPES OF ISSUES THAT CAN BE LOGGED IN RMS
-------------------------------------------------------

=======================================================
A) ACADEMIC & ADMINISTRATIVE CATEGORIES
=======================================================
Used for issues related to academics, classroom activities, or administrative concerns.

1. Hostel Allotment Issues

3. Behaviour of Hostel Staff  
4. Hostel Leave Issues

C) SECURITY & SAFETY ISSUES
=======================================================
• Lost ID Card Reporting

-------------------------------------------------------
4. RMS RESPONSE & RESOLUTION TIMELINE
-------------------------------------------------------
Standard response window:  
➡ **2 to 3 working days**  
(Time may vary depending on workload and availability of staff.)

Maintenance issues like:
• plumbing  
• electrical  
• cooler repairs  
• AC repairs  

are usually resolved faster depending on technician availability.

-------------------------------------------------------
5. IMPORTANT RULES ABOUT RMS
-------------------------------------------------------
• Raise ONE issue per RMS request (don’t mix multiple issues).  
• Provide accurate block & room details.  
• Attach photos for quicker resolution.  
• Avoid disrespectful or abusive language; this may cause disciplinary action.  
• If the issue is resolved, mark the RMS ticket as “Closed.”  
• If not satisfied, mark it as “Reopen” for escalation.

-------------------------------------------------------
6. ESCALATION RULES
-------------------------------------------------------
If an RMS issue is not resolved within a reasonable timeframe:

LEVEL 1 → Hostel Warden  
LEVEL 2 → Residential Services Office (Block 32, Window 3)  
LEVEL 3 → Division of Student Relationship  
LEVEL 4 → Higher Authorities (based on severity)

Cases related to:
• Ragging  
• Harassment  
• Safety threats  
• Medical emergencies  

must be reported immediately to warden + emergency numbers (listed in lpu_security.txt).
