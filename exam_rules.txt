EXAM RULES – LOVELY PROFESSIONAL UNIVERSITY (LPU)

1. Mandatory Requirements for Entering Exam Hall:
   • LPU ID Card (Original)
   • Exam Admit Card (Downloaded from UMS)
   • Students without any of these are NOT allowed inside the exam hall.

2. Reporting Time:
   • Students must report at least 15 minutes before the exam.
   • Late entry beyond 15 minutes from exam start time is strictly NOT allowed.

3. Prohibited Items:
   • Mobile phones (switched off or on silent mode – NOT ALLOWED inside)
   • Smartwatches, Bluetooth devices, earphones
   • Notes, papers, books (unless permitted for OPEN BOOK exam)
   • Programmable calculators (normal calculators allowed for specific courses)

4. Seating Procedure:
   • Seating is allocated through digital seating plan.
   • Students must sit on the allotted seat only.

5. ID Verification:
   • Invigilators will verify identity & admit card.
   • Any mismatch of student photograph/details = denied entry.

6. During the Exam:
   • Talking/whispering is strictly prohibited.
   • Students must raise hand to ask anything.
   • No student can leave exam hall in the first 30 minutes.
   • No student can leave exam hall in the last 15 minutes.

7. Use of Calculator:
   • Only non-programmable calculators are allowed.
   • Calculator sharing is NOT allowed.

8. Answer Sheet Instructions:
   • Fill details exactly as instructed.
   • Do not write irrelevant content or identification marks.
   • Rough work must be done in the provided rough sheet only.

9. Cheating / Misconduct (UMC):
   Any of the following leads to a University Management Case (UMC):
   • Using mobile phone in exam hall
   • Copying from another student
   • Carrying written slips/notes
   • Passing notes to others
   • Talking intentionally
   • Using unfair means during online exams

   Punishments may include:
   • Cancellation of exam
   • “UMC” remark on transcript 
   • Suspension (case-by-case)
   • Zero marks in the course

10. Reappear Exam:
   • Fee: ₹500 per course
   • Internal marks remain same
   • Only end-term marks are replaced
   • Missing the reappear exam = no second chance

11. Open Book Exams (if applicable):
   • Only printed books/notes allowed.
   • Digital devices still banned.

12. Students with Disabilities:
   • Extra time and scribe allowed as per guidelines.
   • Must apply through UMS well before the exams.

13. Important Student Responsibilities:
   • Check the exam date sheet regularly on UMS.
   • Keep ID and admit card ready before exam week.
   • Report any exam clash or discrepancy through RMS.
